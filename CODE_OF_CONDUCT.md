# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and educational environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to use TheWatcher responsibly and ethically.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

✅ Using only for authorized security testing
✅ Obtaining written consent before testing
✅ Respecting privacy laws and regulations
✅ Reporting issues responsibly
✅ Providing constructive feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

❌ Unauthorized surveillance or stalking
❌ Testing without permission
❌ Sharing captured data publicly
❌ Using for malicious purposes
❌ Harassing or threatening others

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Violations will result in:
1. Warning
2. Temporary ban
3. Permanent ban
4. Legal notification (if laws violated)
